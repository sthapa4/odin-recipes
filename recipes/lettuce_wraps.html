Lettuce Wraps

Description

Meaty and leafy goodness that isn't that bad for your health

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 3/4 cup chopped onions
  • 6 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 lbs ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Green Leaf lettuce bunch

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil on a pan over medium heat. Saute the onions until they start to brown slightly. Then add the minced garlic and freshly grated ginger. saute for less than a minute.
  2. Dump in the ground chicken in the pan and cook until lightly brown . Add the hoisin, sriracha and soy sauce. Then let the sauces incorporate with the chicken for a minute. Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve the now cooked ground chicken in lettuce wraps.
