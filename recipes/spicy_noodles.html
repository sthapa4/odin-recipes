Spicy Noodles

Description

Tastes so good

Ingredients

  • Samyang spicy noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a medium sized pan. Boil the ramen in the boiling water for 5 min
  2. Then strain the ramen and add the ramen to a bowl. open the sauce packet it comes with and add the fire sauce to your liking.
  3. Top it off with the seaweed flakes and sesame seed the ramen comes with
